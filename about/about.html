Who we are

Dance-Inn Bangalore is a one of a kind dance studio & a tastefully designed creator's space. We believe in providing rich & efficient dance education for people from all walks of life irrespective of their age and experience.

We strive to offer a positive and uplifting environment for all dancers to train and have a great learning & performing experience.

What Do We Provide

We concentrate on basics, techniques, musicality, body strengthening, free styling & most importantly performing while keeping the fun of learning an art form thriving. We provide classes for various dance forms with specifically skilled & efficient trainers who are not only sound in their foundations but also passionate & patient teachers.
